About

The person behind the p-values

I'm Alen — a biostatistician and data scientist based in Salt Lake City, UT. With a Master's in Biostatistics from the University of Utah and 7+ years of independent consulting experience, I help clinicians and research teams navigate everything from study design to publication-ready analysis.

I've contributed to work published in JAMA Network Open, Neurology, Stroke, and more — and I've delivered invited lectures at Yale University and University of Utah Departments of Neurology since 2021.

Currently, I also lead the Health Data Science Training Program under NIH's AIM-AHEAD initiative, mentoring the next generation of researchers in R, Python, and clinical data science.

Outside the office: avid skier and golfer, currently learning the ukulele 🤙

Services

From A to Z

I adapt to your tools and your timeline — whether you need a one-time analysis or ongoing support throughout your study.

Clinical Trial Design

Power calculations, randomization schemes, adaptive designs, and SAP development to ensure statistical rigor and regulatory alignment from day one.

Statistical Analysis

Regression, survival analysis, mixed models, propensity scores, causal inference, and more — tailored to your research question and data structure.

Data Management & CDISC

CDISC-compliant data pipelines, reproducible workflows, and regulatory-ready datasets to support grant submissions and FDA filings.

Machine Learning & AI

Predictive modeling, time series, NLP, and ML pipelines on clinical and real-world data using Python and R, with production-ready deployment.

Manuscript & Grant Support

Statistical methods sections, results write-ups, tables and figures, and peer review response support for journals and federal grant agencies.

Education & Training

Statistical seminars for clinicians, students, and research teams — from hypothesis testing basics to causal inference and survival analysis.
